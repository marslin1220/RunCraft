### Description

```
RunCraft turns a single race goal into a 16-week structured training plan — paced to your exact fitness level, coached by Jack Daniels' VDOT methodology, and delivered to your Apple Watch automatically.

One-time purchase. All future updates free.

─── YOUR PLAN ───

Enter a recent race time and RunCraft calculates your VDOT — a precise measure of your current running fitness. Every session in your 16-week plan (base → build → peak → taper) is paced to that number: easy runs at true recovery pace, tempo runs at lactate threshold, intervals at VO₂max effort. As your fitness improves, pace targets update automatically.

─── APPLE WATCH ───

Tap Start on any session and RunCraft auto-launches on your Apple Watch — no wrist interaction required. The Watch guides you through every interval with voice announcements and haptic cues, displays live heart rate against your target zone, and saves the completed workout to Apple Health when you're done.

─── INSIGHTS ───

VDOT trend, VO₂max estimate, LT1/LT2 lactate threshold, running economy, heart rate recovery, training zone distribution, weekly mileage, and predicted race times for 5K / 10K / half marathon / marathon — all computed from your Health data, all on-device.

─── BUILT FOR SERIOUS RUNNERS ───

· 10 structured presets: Yasso 800s, Mona Fartlek, Michigan Workout, Cruise Intervals, Hill Repeats, Speed Pyramid, Ladder, Progression Run, Tempo Run, Easy Recovery
· Custom workout editor: build warmup → repeat groups → cooldown with precise distance or time targets and pace zone assignments
· Session substitution: long-press any planned session to swap it for an approved equivalent (e.g. Intervals → Hill Intervals or Fartlek) without disrupting your training week structure
· HRV-aware recovery: when your morning heart rate variability or sleep quality drops, RunCraft flags today's hard session and suggests a downgrade
· Live Activity: pace, heart rate, and interval progress visible on your iPhone lock screen and Dynamic Island throughout every workout
· Home Screen & Lock Screen widget showing today's session at a glance — tap to start directly on Watch without opening the app
· Siri & Apple Intelligence: "Start today's session in RunCraft", "What's today's training?", "Set my VDOT to 52", "Log a run in RunCraft"

─── PRIVACY ───

No accounts. No servers. No analytics. Your training data lives on your iPhone and Apple Watch — nowhere else.

Available in English · 繁體中文 · 简体中文 · 日本語 · 한국어
```

### 描述
```
輸入一場近期成績，RunCraft 計算出你的 VDOT，並生成完整 16 週訓練計畫——基礎期、建構期、巔峰期、減量期，每堂課的配速都精準對應你當前的體能水準，隨著體能提升自動更新。

一次買斷，所有更新永久免費。

─── 你的訓練計畫 ───

依據 Jack Daniels VDOT 方法論，輕鬆跑、節奏跑、間歇跑各有明確配速目標。訓練計畫會隨著你的 VDOT 進步自動更新配速，不需要手動調整。

─── Apple Watch ───

點一下「開始」，Apple Watch 自動啟動訓練——無需操作手錶。語音報告每個間歇的開始與結束，即時顯示心率區間與配速偏差，訓練結束後自動儲存至 Apple 健康。

─── 數據洞察 ───

VDOT 趨勢、VO₂max 估算、LT1/LT2 乳酸閾值、跑步經濟性、心率恢復、訓練區間分布、週里程、5K/10K/半馬/全馬預測成績——全部來自 Apple 健康資料，在本機運算，不上傳任何資料。

─── 認真跑者的工具 ───

· 10 種結構化訓練：Yasso 800s、Mona Fartlek、Michigan Workout 等
· 自訂訓練編輯器：暖身＋反覆組合＋緩和，精確設定距離、時間與配速區間
· 長按訓練卡片可替換為同類型等效訓練（例如間歇→山坡間歇），不破壞訓練週結構
· HRV 感知恢復：晨間心率變異率偏低時，主動提示今日降強度
· 鎖定畫面 Live Activity：隨時看到配速、心率、間歇進度
· 主畫面 Widget：一眼看今天練什麼，點一下直接送 Watch 開始訓練
· Siri 整合：「用 RunCraft 開始今天的訓練」「RunCraft 今天練什麼」

─── 隱私 ───

無帳號、無伺服器、無任何資料追蹤。你的訓練資料只存在你的 iPhone 和 Apple Watch 上。
```
